How to install

Follow these simple steps to get started.

1

Open the installer

Tap the downloaded file. When prompted, choose Package installer to continue.

2

Tap Install

When you see Do you want to install this app?, tap Install.

3

Wait for installation

Wait a moment while ySpace installs on your device.

4

Open ySpace

Once you see App installed, tap Open to launch ySpace.
